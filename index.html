Scarborough Area

Scarborough is one of Perth's most iconic beachside suburbs and is located approximately 14 kilometers from the Perth CBD. It is surrounded by world-renowned beaches, sunny eateries and amenities making it the ideal location for those craving to be a hop away from exciting entertainment and recreation. With nearby shops, sunshine and a bustling nightlife, you can bring your coastal dreams to life living in a Nicheliving Scarborough home.

  • Quality Education: Our Nicheliving Scarborough homes sit within the catchment area of a selection of high-quality education facilities. These include the highly respected Scarborough Primary School, St John's Primary School, St Mary's Anglican Girls' School, Hale School, and Churchlands Senior High School.
  • Shop Till you Drop: Our Nicheliving Scarborough homes are only a short drive away from one of Perth's best shopping destinations ensuring that all your necessities will be at your doorstep. Just 4 minutes from Coles and 7 minutes away from the newly refurbished Karrinyup Shopping Centre, you have a range of grocery, fashion, and lifestyle brands close to home.
  • Healthy Lifestyle: Get in touch with nature with glistening oceans and luscious reserves surrounding your new home. Enjoy a short 8 minute walk down to the stunning seaside which is home to Scarborough Beach Pools, Scarborough Beach Skate Park, Whale Playground, and a rock- climbing wall. As well as the kids, your fury friends will be happy with Peasholm and Trigg dog beaches both being within 6 minutes from home, so the whole family can enjoy a spacious coastal run.
  • Brunch, & Dine: Enjoy coffee dates and foreshore catch ups every day of the week with Scarborough's famous Esplanade strip just an incredible 3 minute walk away from home. You will be spoilt for choice with plenty of cafes and bars to choose from. Watch the stunning sunset over a cocktail at El Grotto and Peach Pit or play a game of bowling at The Lookout. If you prefer brunch, head to Lady Latte, Doric Street Cafe and Kitchen or The Wild Fig for coffee, all within 6 minutes from your new home.

Nicheliving Willetton

Willetton is the perfect example of a classic Perth suburb, offering the warmth and safety of a family friendly neighbourhood just 15 kilometres from the vibrant hub that is our ever-evolving CBD.

The suburb began to flourish in the 1970s and has shown strong, steady growth ever since, making it one of Australia’s most consistent suburbs for property growth. Given the area’s maturity, you can expect cosy streets lined with leafy trees and neighbours comprised of middle income earners in established couples and family groups. It’s also a dynamic cultural pocket known for its diversity, with 1 in 5 residents hailing from South East Asia.

It’s hardly surprising that this suburb is in high demand, especially given the internationally recognised education facilities in the area, with Nicheliving Willetton located within the highly-regarded Willetton Senior High School zone. Just look at all that surrounds Willetton:

  • Education: The suburb is home to Willetton Senior High School, one of the state’s top secondary schools, as well as other prestigious, high performing primary and high schools. Nestled directly between Curtin and Murdoch universities, Willetton is also the ideal spot for any seeking tertiary education.
  • Shopping: Southlands Boulevard and Stockland Riverton sit right on your doorstep, or if you’re looking to splurge at major brands, you’ve got some of WA’s largest shopping malls in Carousel Shopping Centre and Garden City.
  • Recreation & Lifestyle: Willetton is within easy reach of some of Perth’s most iconic landmarks and venues. For those who lead an active lifestyle, there’s Riverton Leisureplex, Willetton Sports Club, Willetton Basketball Association as well as multiple lush golf courses and parks.
  • Transport: Connectivity is one of the greatest advantages of Willetton. Bordered by three major road networks in the Kwinana Freeway, Leach Highway and Roe Highway, and located next to several train station, travelling to any part of the metro area can be done with ease.
  • Health: For a general check-up you have the Willetton Medical Centre and for more specialised healthcare there’s the state of the art Fiona Stanley Hospital.
